Nervous is the treasurer and co-founder of RDC. Her last name fits her well, considering she is often childish. She is always in a constant state of nervousness which earned her the nickname Nervous.

She has a difficult time showing her personality and often clings to the closest person due to her sidekick syndrome. Nervous has one pet cat, but she doesn't keep track of it very well. The cat's name is also Yandy, like Duck's cat, but they are not the same cat.
