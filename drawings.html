FLOATING

The original artwork is red and black ink on paper. I wanted to create a weightless feeling as if she were floating, the focus point being the dramatically large eyes.

The second work is a semi-glitched version. Most of the original color and shape is still visible.

The third work is the final glitched image. The eyes have completely vanished and the color is fully distorted.
