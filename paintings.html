"Purple", Tempura Painting on Paper, 12"x18", 2020

This was my first time using tempura paint. I wanted to try a complimentary color scheme. I used mostly purples and yellows for this piece.
"Blue", Tempura Painting on Paper, 12"x18", 2020

This was my second attempt using the tempura paints. For this piece, I chose my favorite complimentary colors, orange and blue. I didn't want to restrict myself to just two colors, so I included hints of greens, yellows, and pinks as well.

"Landscape", Black Ink on Paper, 12"x18", 2017

This is an ink painting. I used three separate references and rearranged the scenery to create a new landscape. The waterfall, palmtrees, and buildings are taken from separate photos.
"Self Portrait", Black Ink on Paper, 6"x 8", 2017

This is a quick self-portrait. I drew a sketch of myself by the pool, using a reference photo. This is one of three self-portraits and the most recent.
"Malicay", Red and Black Ink on Paper, 6"x 8", 2017

I wanted to gift two of my friends paintings. This is an ink painting of my friend from high school. She is currently a singer and she goes by Malicay. I used a photo of her for reference and created a monochromatic painting for her. I used a bold and exciting red to accent the painting. As an artist, she inspires me a lot. I've used her photos for other references as well.
