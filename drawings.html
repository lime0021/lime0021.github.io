"Color of Music", Pen and Crayon Drawing, 2016

I enjoy listening to music because it makes life more colorful. I wanted experiment more with color by express the way music can enhance my emotions.
"Shadows", Pen Drawing, 2016

I wanted to experiment more with the deep contrasts and contours of the face. I used a ballpoint pen to get the basic shapes of the face. Later, I went through with a sharpie to fill in the shadows.
"Receipts", Pen Drawings, 2019-2020

I work part time at a grocery store. I've been working there for over a year now. When I'm not busy, I take little pieces of receipt paper and whatever pens are nearby to draw. This is a chronological collection of all of the receipt drawings that I have kept.
